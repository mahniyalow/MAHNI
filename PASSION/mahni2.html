"MAHNI / 많이"

What is MAHNI?"

"많이" is a korean term, which translates to "many." it is pronounced "MAH-NI."

MAHNI is a clothing brand that targets dancers and fashion lovers as a whole. As a dancer with interest in fashion, I wanted to a brand that reflect dancing in;

    * movement
    * silhouette

and

    * identity

My goal is to represent and share how we as individuals are not made to fit just one subject or style, but we are made to fit many movements.
